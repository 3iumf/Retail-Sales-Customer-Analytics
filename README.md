Retail Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover insights about revenue performance, product profitability, regional trends, and customer behavior.

The analysis transforms raw transactional data into business intelligence using Excel, MySQL, and Power BI.

Tools Used

Excel – Data cleaning and exploration
MySQL – Data modeling and analytical queries
Power BI – Interactive dashboard visualization


Dashboard Preview




Key Business Insights

- Technology products generate the highest revenue and profit across all product categories, indicating that this category is the primary driver of business growth.
- The Central region shows the lowest profit margin despite strong sales performance due to higher average discounts compared to other regions.
- Tables represent the least profitable product sub-category and generate negative profit, suggesting pricing or supply chain issues.
- Customer analysis reveals that a small group of high-value customers contributes a large portion of total revenue.
- Using RFM segmentation, customers were classified into Champions, Loyal Customers, Potential Loyalists, and At-Risk segments to support targeted marketing strategies.

Dashboard Features

The dashboard provides insights into:

• Sales performance by product category
• Profitability by region
• Revenue trends over time
• Product sub-category profitability
• Customer segmentation using RFM analysis
• Customer value matrix (Sales vs Profit)
